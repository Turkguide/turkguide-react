Overview

TurkGuide helps users discover Turkish businesses and professionals and interact with community content. This Privacy Policy explains what data we may process and your choices.

Data We May Process

How We Use Data

  • To provide and maintain TurkGuide features.
  • To improve safety, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • To respond to support requests.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only as needed to provide the service or comply with law.

Your Choices
